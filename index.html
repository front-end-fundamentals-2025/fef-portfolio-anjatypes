Meet the Designer

Introducing Me

My name is Anja Hallberg and I am 23 years old. I am currently a full time student in the program of Graphic Design and Web Development (NMD) at Jönköping University. This website is a part of an assignment where the goal is to create a page about myself. My biggest hobby, aside from graphic design and code, of course, is strength training. I love going to the gym and I am shamelessly making it my whole personality. Nothing beats a good gym session or learning all the scientific stuff behind how exercise affects the body and mind. Whenever I get the opportunity to travel I like to live and explore as if I were a local resident, that's how you find the best spots!
